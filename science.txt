SCIENCE:

A Brief History of Time 
Author: Stephen Hawking
This bestseller explores the nature of time, the universe, and black holes, written for a general audience.

Cosmos
Author: Carl Sagan
A classic that takes readers on a journey through space and time, exploring the wonders of the universe.

The Selfish Gene
Author: Richard Dawkins
Dawkins introduces the concept of the gene as a unit of natural selection, shaping our understanding of evolution.

Silent Spring 
Author: Rachel Carson
A groundbreaking work that raised awareness about the environmental impact of pesticides, leading to the modern environmental movement.

The Double Helix 
Author: James D. Watson
This book recounts the discovery of the structure of DNA and the race to uncover the genetic code.

The Origin of Species
Author: Charles Darwin
Darwin's seminal work that laid the foundation for the theory of evolution by natural selection.

G del, Escher, Bach: An Eternal Golden Braid 
Author: Douglas Hofstadter
An interdisciplinary exploration of mathematics, art, and music, delving into the nature of human thought.

The Elegant Universe 
Author: Brian Greene
Greene explains the complex theories of string theory and the nature of the universe in an accessible way.

Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind
Author: Yuval Noah Harari
While not strictly a science book, it explores the history of Homo sapiens from a scientific and anthropological perspective.

The Immortal Life of Henrietta Lacks
Author: Rebecca Skloot
Blending science and narrative, this book tells the story of Henrietta Lacks and her immortal cells, which have been crucial in medical research.
